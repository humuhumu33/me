## Executive summary

Ilya Paveliev builds and backs companies at the intersection of **deep tech, artificial intelligence, and capital markets**. He is the co-founder of **Hologram Technologies**, a software-defined compute platform for local AI inference, and a founding partner of **Arete Capital**, a $20m hybrid venture fund backed by a16z principals.

His career spans $20bn institutional asset management, market-neutral digital-asset trading, and bulge-bracket M&A — a rare combination of operator, allocator, and engineer.
